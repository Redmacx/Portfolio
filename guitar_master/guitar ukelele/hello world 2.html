Who said Metallica can't be played on ukulele? Watch this awesome arrangement and learn to play it yourself!

🪕 Ukulele Cover — Full Tutorial

🎼

Key & Tuning

Standard ukulele tuning (GCEA) — adapted from the original E minor key.

⭐

Difficulty

Medium — more accessible than the guitar version, but still requires accurate picking.

🪕

Instrument

Works best on a soprano or concert ukulele. Tenor ukulele recommended for lower notes.

💡

Tip

The main riff translates surprisingly well! Focus on clean fingerpicking for the best tone.
